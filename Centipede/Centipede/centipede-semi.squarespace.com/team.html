Mr. Jonathan Friedmann, Ph.D. is Centipede's CEO, Co-Founder. Prior to Centipede he served as the COO and VP R&D of Provigent and Senior Directorat Broadcom. Previously, he held various engineering management roles at Metalink. Mr. Friedmann holds a Ph.D. in Statistical Signal Processing from Tel Aviv University, M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Industrial Engineering both Magna Cum Laude from Tel Aviv University.

Mr. Alberto Mandler (Tito) is Centipede's VP R&D, Co-Founder. Prior to Centipede he served as an Architecture and VLSI consultant at multiple companies including, TI, Coppergate and Primesense. Mr. Mandler served as a key chip design team manager at Analog Devices developing the TigerShark and managed the VLSI team at Ramon-Chips. He holds a B.Sc. in Aero-space Engineering from the Technion, Haifa.

Mr. Noam Mizrahi is Centipede's CTO, Co-Founder. Prior to Centipede he served as a System Architect at Broadcom leading SoC the CPU & DSP Multi-core subsystem. Previously, held various leading architecture roles at Provigent, Coppergate, ASOCS and Intel. Holds a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Tel Aviv University.

Mr. Eli Harry  is Centipede's Executive Chairman, Co-Founder. Prior to Centipede he served as Executive Chairman in Provigent which was acquired by Broadcom. Mr. Harry was the Co-Founder & CEO of LanOptics and took the company public (Nasdaq). He holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science, Magna Cum Laude from the Technion, Haifa.
